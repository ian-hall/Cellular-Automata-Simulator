Ian's cellular automata thing

What was once a basic program written for CS101 that went through a few generations of Conway's Game of Life has turned into this program for simulating various types of cellular automata. Written in C#, I have harnessed the power of the console to bring a robust, full-featured, retro-styled, buzzword described program that lets users choose between types, rule sets, and starting populations. You can find it here on GitHub.

Just look at these awesome features:


Full-featured menus



Supports loading of CELLS-style files of populations



Robust error handling



Choose from a wide selection of premade populations



Advanced population creation functionality



Also supports 1D automata
